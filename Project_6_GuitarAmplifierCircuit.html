Guitar Amplifier Circuit Project Description

Over the summer of 2017, I decided to design and build my own guitar amplifier using vacuum tubes, which are the components used in high end vintage-style amplifiers. After doing extensive research, I was interested to learn a few things about the project:

  • A present circuit can deliver 10W of output power when driving an 8 Ohm load.
  • Standard Amplifier Circuits feature a two-FET amplifier, two inputs with different sensitivity, a treble-cut control and an optional switch allowing overdrive or powerful treble-enhancement.
